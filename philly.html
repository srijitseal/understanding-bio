June 4 Program

This special Understanding.bio gathering brings together pharmaceutical and academic perspectives on how AI is changing drug discovery, medicinal chemistry, automation, and translational decision-making.

Featured speakers: Dr. Matt McDonald (Drexel University), Dr. Xiang Yu (Eli Lilly), Dr. Deidre Dalmas (Boehringer Ingelheim), and Dr. Vladimir Chupakhin (Bristol Myers Squibb).

What to Expect

Four 45-minute sessions will run through the afternoon, with a networking break between the second and third talks. The program is designed for students and researchers who want practical exposure to AI-enabled pharmaceutical discovery across industry and academia.

Program Themes

  • AI and machine learning in pharma research workflows
  • Computational chemistry, medicinal chemistry, and toxicology applications
  • Automation, data-driven discovery, and translational decision-making
  • Student networking with regional industry and academic scientists

About Understanding.bio

Understanding.bio is Philadelphia's community for students and professionals working at the intersection of artificial intelligence and drug discovery. Founded in 2025, we are growing to become a hub for collaboration, innovation, and knowledge sharing in the Greater Philadelphia biotech ecosystem.

Our Community

Our membership includes computational biologists, medicinal chemists, data scientists, clinicians, pharmaceutical researchers, biotech entrepreneurs, and students from across the Philadelphia region. We welcome professionals from all career stages, from graduate students to industry veterans.

Meeting Format

Each quarterly meeting typically features:

• Technical talks from academia and industry
• Q&A and moderated discussion
• Networking and informal collaboration time
• Student-facing exposure to regional drug discovery careers

Philadelphia's AI Drug Discovery Ecosystem

Philadelphia is uniquely positioned as a leader in AI-driven drug discovery, with world-class research institutions, major pharmaceutical companies, and a growing startup ecosystem. Our meetings leverage this rich environment to foster innovation and collaboration.

Values and Principles

We are committed to open science, reproducible research, ethical AI development, and inclusive collaboration. Our community prioritizes rigorous scientific discourse while maintaining a welcoming environment for all participants.

Meetings & Events

Understanding.bio hosts meetings featuring cutting-edge research presentations, technical discussions, and networking opportunities. All meetings are free and open to the community.
