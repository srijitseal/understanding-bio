Welcome to Understanding.bio

Understanding.bio represents Philadelphia's vibrant drug discovery AI community, bringing together interdisciplinary experts from academia, industry, and healthcare institutions across the Greater Philadelphia area.

Our Mission: To accelerate therapeutic discovery and development through collaborative AI research, knowledge sharing, and innovative partnerships that transform how we understand and treat human disease.

What We Do

Our monthly meetings feature presentations on breakthrough AI methodologies in drug discovery, from molecular design and target identification to clinical trial optimization. We explore machine learning applications in pharmacokinetics, toxicology prediction, and personalized medicine approaches.

About Understanding.bio

Understanding.bio is Philadelphia's premier community for professionals working at the intersection of artificial intelligence and drug discovery. Founded in 2024, we've grown to become a vital hub for collaboration, innovation, and knowledge sharing in the Greater Philadelphia biotech ecosystem.

Our Community

Our diverse membership includes computational biologists, medicinal chemists, data scientists, clinicians, pharmaceutical researchers, biotech entrepreneurs, and students from across the Philadelphia region. We welcome professionals from all career stages, from graduate students to industry veterans.

Meeting Format

Each monthly meeting typically features:

• Two 25-minute technical presentations
• 15-minute Q&A and discussion sessions
• 30-minute networking reception
• Informal discussions and collaboration opportunities

Philadelphia's AI Drug Discovery Ecosystem

Philadelphia is uniquely positioned as a leader in AI-driven drug discovery, with world-class research institutions, major pharmaceutical companies, and a growing startup ecosystem. Our meetings leverage this rich environment to foster innovation and collaboration.

Key Institutions: University of Pennsylvania, Children's Hospital of Philadelphia, Drexel University, Temple University, Thomas Jefferson University, and numerous pharmaceutical and biotech companies.

Values and Principles

We are committed to open science, reproducible research, ethical AI development, and inclusive collaboration. Our community prioritizes rigorous scientific discourse while maintaining an welcoming environment for all participants.
